Cultural Event
EVENT NAME :
Upside Down Rhythms : Waves of Rhythm,Steps of grace
DESCRIPTION :
The Cultural Runway is a vibrant celebration that blends the elegance of a fashion
showcase with the energy of dance. Each team represents an Indian state or cultural
theme through authentic traditional attire and expressive performances.
The event begins with participants walking the ramp, presenting the beauty and heritage of
their cultural costumes. This is followed by a lively dance performance that reflects the
music, spirit, and identity of their chosen tradition. Bringing together fashion, rhythm, and
culture on one stage, The Cultural Runway celebrates Indiaâ€™s unity in diversity in a colorful
and captivating showcase.
NUMBER OF ROUNDS : 1 Round
TEAM SIZE :
Minimum: 6 members
Maximum: 10 members
RULES AND REGULATIONS TO BE FOLLOWED:
1. A team can consist of minimum 6 and maximum 10 members.
2. The total time limit is 5 minutes (1 minute for stage preparation and 4 minutes for
performance).
3. Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
4. Audio should be submitted to the desk prior to the performance in a pen drive. A copy must
be submitted 3 days prior to the event.
5. Props are allowed, but they must be safe and easy to handle.
6. Use of fire, water, fireworks, glass, sharp objects, or any hazardous materials is strictly
prohibited.
7. Costumes should be decent and appropriate as per college/school guidelines.
8. Vulgar moves, inappropriate expressions, or offensive content are strictly prohibited.
9. Participants must maintain discipline on and off the stage.
10. All participants must report at least 30 minutes before the event.
